Who Are We?

This website is a medium for Queen’s University students to search through a more comprehensive list of Arts and Science online courses. By being able to filter by a course’s syllabus breakdown (ex. group work, exams), students will be able to cater their university education to their needs and wants.

After choosing the level of group work you wish to have and whether or not you want an in-person exam, just click 'Submit' and our database will prompt a table with all relevant results to your search! Along with the course code and units earned, the course name and term dates will also be shown.

Good luck with course selection!
