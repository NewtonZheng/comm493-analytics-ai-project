Who Are We?

This website is a medium for Queen’s University students to search through a more comprehensive list of Arts and Science online courses. By being able to filter by a course’s syllabus breakdown (ex. group work, exams), students will be able to cater their university education to their needs and wants.

After choosing the level of group work you wish to have and whether or not you want an in-person exam, just click 'Submit' and our website will load all the relevant courses for your search! The table below is provided as an example with a sample of the courses that has more information such as the course name, the term, and the direct links to the official Queen's online courses website.
